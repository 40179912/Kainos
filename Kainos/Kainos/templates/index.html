Data X

Data X is a special system designed to help you with your problems involving large data. Thanks to this webiste you will be able to take either your own datasets or a open data dataset and both manipulate them and visualize them.

Insert dataset

From here you can insert your very own dataset, whether it be from your own computer or one that you found online. This will store your dataset for future use.

Insert Dataset »

Download dataset

From here you can download any dataset that you've inserted or altered into your system.

Download Dataset »

Alter Dataset

Here you can take the datasets you have provided and alter them in whatever way you see fit. You can change the conents of the dataset, create a new dataset by merging 2 of your datasets together or flatten certain data if you desire.

Manipulate Dataset »

Visualise Dataset

Here you can visualise your dataset into whatever form you desire. If you want to create a graph based on your numbers, you can do so. If you want to put some coordinates on a map you can do so.
